About

Tang Lab focuses on translational biomedical imaging technologies, with an emphasis on optical coherence tomography (OCT) and its clinical applications in organ transplantation and disease assessment.

Research

  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)
  • Polarization-sensitive OCT (PS-OCT)
  • Dynamic OCT and functional imaging
  • Transplant organ viability assessment
